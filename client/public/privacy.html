Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Zynk. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use the Zynk mobile application and website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username, optionally your phone number or email, and a password.
  • Profile Information: You may provide a display name and profile picture.
  • Messages and Media: We process your messages, photos, videos, and audio recordings to deliver them to your intended recipients.
  • Contacts: With your permission, we may access your contacts to help you find friends on Zynk.
  • Device Information: We collect basic device data (like OS version and push notification tokens) to ensure the app functions correctly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to operate, maintain, and improve the Zynk service. This includes:

  • Delivering messages and notifications.
  • Facilitating audio and video calls.
  • Providing AI-powered features like chat summaries.
  • Keeping your account secure.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information in the following circumstances:

  • With other users: Information you share in chats (messages, media, profile info) is visible to the people you communicate with.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (like Google Gemini API for summaries) to power specific features. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We store your messages and account data as long as your account is active. You can delete your messages at any time. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@zynk.app.
